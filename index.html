BoT Resolution Process

A comprehensive, end-to-end workflow that streamlines the development, review, and approval of resolutions originating from campus-level or internal OAA offices through final Board of Trustees (BoT) approval. Featuring AI-powered content creation, this process enhances the accuracy of resolutions, accelerates review and approval timelines, and reinforces standardized practices across OAA. It also enables transparent tracking and monitoring, ensuring greater efficiency, consistency, and accountability throughout the resolution lifecycle.

OSP Yearly Report

A structured end-to-end digital workflow that guides campuses in compiling and submitting the annual program report using the CD-SEEK template. It captures all required sections — executive summary, program overview, budgets, outcomes, and oversight — and supports e-signature and automatic PDF generation to ensure compliance with state requirements, standardize report creation, accelerate review cycles, and provide transparency and accountability.

Intake Form

This process focuses on developing a project intake form that undergoes an initial, iterative review and feedback cycle before progressing to the governance stage for approval.
